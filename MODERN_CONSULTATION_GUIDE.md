## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:

**Q: My draft wasn't saved**
- Check internet connection
- Verify auto-save indicator shows recent save time
- Drafts expire after 24 hours

**Q: Medication suggestions not appearing**
- Ensure diagnosis is entered correctly
- Check spelling of condition
- Some conditions may not have pre-configured suggestions

**Q: Form not submitting**
- Check for required fields (marked with *)
- Verify all validation errors are resolved
- Check error messages at top of form

**Q: Template not applying correctly**
- Close and reopen the wizard
- Clear browser cache if persistent
- Try selecting template again

**Q: Can't find specialist form**
- Use search function
- Check filter settings (role, active status)
- Verify form is marked as "active" in system
